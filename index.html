About Me:

Hey there, I’m Paige! I’m a fourth year student at the University of Colorado Boulder studying computer science and marketing. Here at CU I am a member of programs such as the Leeds Scholars Program and Presidents Leadership Class, as well as clubs such as Leeds Consulting Group.

I love product design becasue it is the perfect combination of art, business, and engineering. It has given me opportunities to find creative ways to help others and solve problems.

Outside of work and school, I am a huge book and movie nerd. I am also a Colorado Native, and I love to stay active and get outdoors. I love to snowboard, hike, and go paddle boarding. I also love to travel have been to 23 national parks and 16 countries! In fact, in 2023, I lived abroad in Sweden while studying at Uppsala University for a semester.
